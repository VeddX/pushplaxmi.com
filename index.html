About Our Factory

DIVYAKIRTI is a trusted consumer brand manufactured and managed under our corporate parent enterprise, Pushplaxmi Technopure Pvt Limited. We operate a highly hygienic, fully mechanical offline factory focused on providing nutritious kitchen flours to households and wholesalers alike.

By blending traditional stone-ground milling principles with state-of-the-art cleaning machinery, we ensure that every batch retains its natural grain fibers, core nutrients, and authentic taste profile.
